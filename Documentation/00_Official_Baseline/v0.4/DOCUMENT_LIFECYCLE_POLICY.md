# Living Documentation Policy

1. Every sprint closure runs a document impact review.
2. Affected documents increment version and receive evidence-based updates.
3. Reviewed documents with no impact retain their version and are listed as `Reviewed - No Change`.
4. Previous official baselines are immutable.
5. Operational records may change during a sprint; an official baseline is published only at sprint closure or major milestone.
6. No document may claim implementation without QA or repository evidence.
7. Every new baseline includes changelog, impact matrix, manifest, integrity report and SHA-256 checksums.
