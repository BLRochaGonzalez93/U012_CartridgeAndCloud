# ADR-0020 — Initial Store Shell Dimensions and Entrance

**Status:** Accepted  
**Sprint:** 5  
**Decision date:** 2026-06-25

## Decision

The initial playable Store shell uses:

- Interior width: `10 m`.
- Interior depth: `15 m`.
- Logical dimensions: `20 x 30` cells.
- Cell size: `0.5 m`.
- Central entrance width: `2 m` / four cells.
- Wall height: `3 m`.
- Wall thickness: `0.2 m`.

The entrance remains centered on the front wall and connects to an exterior
technical apron.

## Rationale

The dimensions provide enough area to validate movement, camera behaviour,
construction and route preservation while remaining appropriate for the first
Store tier and the project scope.

## Consequences

- Store world and logical-grid dimensions align exactly.
- The initial shell is fixed for the accepted Sprint 5 baseline.
- Store expansion remains a future system.
